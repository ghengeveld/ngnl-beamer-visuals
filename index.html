Keyboard shortcuts (sides and TV only)

Space Stop / Start slideshow.
Left arrow Previous slide.
Right arrow Next slide.
1 Sides: Switch to sponsors slide (blue) and stop.
TV: Switch to welcome slide (red/white/blue) and stop.
2 Sides: Switch to welcome/twitter slide (red) and stop.
TV: Switch to twitter slide (red) and stop.
3 TV: Switch to app slide (red/white/blue) and stop.
4 TV: Switch to sponsors slide (blue) and stop.
+ Increase slideshow interval (go faster) by 10 seconds.
- Decrease slideshow interval (go faster) by 10 seconds.

The default slideshow interval is 60 seconds. The minimum is 10 seconds.
